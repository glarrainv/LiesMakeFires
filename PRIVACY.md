# Privacy Policy

**Extension:** Lies Make Fires
**Author:** Gaspar Larrain
**Effective date:** 06/18/2026
**Last updated:** 06/18/2026

Lies Make Fires (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") is a browser extension that surfaces verifiable, scientifically sound environmental information directly within Google Search. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what the extension accesses, hand how the data is handled within a user's device.

---

## 1. Summary of Data Practices
Lies Make Fires solely accesses and stores data within the user's Chrome browser. There is no connection to external servers in any part of the process.

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does the extension collect personal data? | No data is transmitted off your device. |
| Does it use external servers to function? | No. The classification model runs locally. |
| Does it use cookies, analytics, or ad trackers? | No. |
| Does it sell or share data with third parties? | No. |
| Does it transfer data for advertising or creditworthiness? | No. |
| Where is processing performed? | Browser cache. |

---

## 2. What the Extension Accesses

To do its job, the extension reads and processes certain information **locally, in your browser**:

- **Your search query text.** When you perform a Google Search, the extension reads the text of your query so a local text-classification model can determine whether it is environment-related. Queries that are not environment-related are ignored, so your normal browsing is not disrupted.
- **Website content from search and offloaded tabs.** When a query is identified as environment-related, the extension may access content from the search results and from an offloaded tab in order to retrieve and present accurate environmental information. Under the Chrome Web Store's data categories, this is classified as **Website content** and **User activity**.
- **Your saved preferences.** Any settings you choose are stored locally on your device (for example, via your browser's local extension storage) so the extension behaves the way you expect.

This access is limited to delivering the extension's single purpose: surfacing accurate, verifiable environmental information within Google Search.

---

## 3. How Information Is Used

All of the information above is used **only on your device** and **only** to:

1. Identify whether a search query relates to environmental topics.
2. Retrieve and display accurate, scientifically sound environmental information relevant to that query.
3. Remember your preferences between sessions.

The text-classification model that powers this runs locally. The extension **does not depend on our servers** to function, and we do not receive your queries, your browsing activity, or the content of the pages you visit.

---

## 4. Local Processing and No Data Collection

The extension is designed to keep everything local:

- **No off-device transmission.** Your queries and the page content the extension reads are processed in your browser and are not sent to us or to any backend we operate.
- **No analytics or telemetry.** We do not track usage, install analytics SDKs, or log your activity.
- **No advertising.** The extension contains no advertising partners, web beacons, or third-party tracking scripts.

Because nothing leaves your device, we have nothing to retain, and we never build a profile of you.

---

## 5. Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties, because we do not collect it. A few external services are worth noting for transparency:

- **Google Search.** The extension operates within Google Search. Your interactions with Google Search itself are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy), not by us.
- **Google Translate compatibility.** The extension is designed to work alongside Google Translate. If you use Google Translate, your use of that feature is subject to Google's own privacy practices. The extension does not send your data to Google Translate on your behalf.

We do not control, and are not responsible for, the privacy practices of Google or any other website you visit.

## 6. Data Sharing, Sale, and Limited Use

Consistent with the Chrome Web Store [User Data Policy](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/webstore/program-policies/user-data-faq), including the Limited Use requirements, we affirm that this extension:

- does **not** sell or transfer user data to third parties, except as required for a user-facing feature, for legal reasons, or as part of a merger or acquisition with prior user consent;
- does **not** use or transfer user data for personalized advertising;
- does **not** use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes;
- does **not** allow humans to read user data, except in the narrow cases the policy permits (such as user consent, security, or legal compliance).

Our use of information obtained through the extension is limited to providing and improving its single, user-facing purpose.

---

## 7. Security

We design the extension to minimize risk by keeping processing on your device, which means there is no central store of your data for anyone to breach. Your locally stored preferences remain on your device under your browser's protection. That said, no method of electronic storage is ever 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information held on your own device or transmitted by your browser to services you choose to use.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The extension is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information at all.

---

## 9. Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions needed for its single purpose—reading the search query, accessing search and offloaded-tab content to retrieve environmental information, and storing your preferences locally. It does not request permissions unrelated to this purpose.
